Jodhpur & Udaipur [India 2020]

Travels • Mar 18, 2020

From Jaisalmer, Erwin Makki and I traveled to Jodhpur. It was one of the Rajput kingdoms. It is called the blue city, with most buildings painted in blue. After a day or two, we left for Udaipur, also called the city of lakes. Both were Rajput kingdoms with rich history in the past, and architecture that reflects that.
