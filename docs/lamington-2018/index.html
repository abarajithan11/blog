Lamington Hike [Australia 2018]

Travels • Sep 8, 2018

The best hike I've done in Australia . Hiked the Border Track of Lamington National Park on a rainy day. 17 km, 6 hours, 1000m elevation. About 10 waterfalls within first 3 hours! . Glad it rained, the forest was green and waterfalls had water. The tracks were full of leeches though.

Jimmy called me at 10.30 pm and told "Aba, we are hiking Lamington tomorrow at 5 am. Wanna join?". I was like "Wait, ok". Gotta thank Mikaël for driving us for 4 hours. Also, special thanks to Omar for feeding me after I forgot my lunch and water at home. :-) . Week 11/24
