Best Summer 2017

Stories • Aug 19, 2017

(7,000 km of motorbike, boat, train, bus, ferry, jeep, rosa, bicycle... in sixty days)

Bathed in 7 beaches, Visited two more: Casurina, Deft, Chaddy, Nagarkovil, KKS, Tangalle, Mullaitivu – Mathakal, Manalkadu.

Watched Esala Perahara [hiding from police on a building].

Cycling in Anuradhapura (Ruwanweliseya….) & Mihinthale

Motorbike tours in Mullaitivu (war tourism) and Jaffna

Bible rock Hike

To Badulla (nine arch) and back in 14 hours.

Deniyaya (2 days) + Sinharaja Forest

Stay in high end hotels and resorts: Palm Paradise (Tangalle), Thulasi Mahal (Jaffna)

Robotics workshops in Kanthale, Dambulla, staying 4 days in Matale

Best department trip of all time: Unforgettable Jaffna Experience.

Best AIESEC Trip: Again Jaffna, unforgettable experience…

Mora Exam supervision in Kandy

Earned 6000 from Tution classes

Learnt to swim by myself (freestroke) in the ocean itself!
.
All this within sixty days… 😊 This is the second best summer, next to Vietnam... :-)

A week of roller coaster ride!! #TravelDiaries
.
Train, bus, jeep, boat, motorbike, van.... #feelingAlive #feelingLanka

Day 1: To badulla in train and back in bus at 3 am. (14 hours)

Day 2: Around colombo in day and Night mail to jaffna with department

Day 3: Places, KKs beach, campfire, Authentic jaffna experience

Day 4: Delft, horse ride, Chaddy beach

Day 5. Vallipuram temple, Nagarkovil beach with strong waves that throw u up and down, nallur, RIO

Day 6: Bike rides: north jaffna, casurina beach

Day 7. Movie @ jaffna MC, Bike ride to Poonaryn, return to Colombo

Day 8: To kandy with eps (now). Bible rock hike, pinnawala, kandy esala perahara and back to colombo.

Thanks to Daaru, Praga and friends for the awesome Jaffna experience!
